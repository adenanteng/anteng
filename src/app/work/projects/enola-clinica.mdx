## Overview

Enola Clinica is a comprehensive clinic management system designed to optimize and digitize medical operations. Built to support outpatient, inpatient, emergency, pharmacy, and laboratory services, it provides a seamless experience for both healthcare providers and patients.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Department Support**: Covers Rawat Jalan, Rawat Inap, IGD, Farmasi, Penunjang/Lab, Poli Umum, Poli Gigi, Poli Kebidanan, and KIA.
- **Electronic Medical Records (EMR)**: Secure and efficient patient record management.
- **Pharmacy & Inventory Management**: Ensures stock availability and prescription tracking.
- **Billing & Insurance Integration**: Automates transactions and claim submissions.
- **Appointment Scheduling**: Streamlines patient appointments and doctor availability.
- **Mobile Accessibility**: Enables access to patient data and appointments on the go.
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Provides insights for better decision-making and resource allocation.

## Technologies Used

- **Backend**: Laravel (Filament PHP, Sanctum), FrankenPHP.
- **Frontend**: Alpine.js, Livewire, Tailwind CSS.
- **Database**: MariaDB, Redis.
- **Security**: Role-based authentication & encrypted patient data

## Challenges and Learnings

Building a system that accommodates various medical workflows while maintaining ease of use was a significant
challenge. Balancing security, performance, and user experience required extensive iteration and feedback from
real-world clinics. Integration with SatuSehat also presented technical hurdles, particularly in ensuring smooth data
synchronization with government healthcare systems.

## Outcome

Enola Clinica is now actively used by healthcare providers, reducing administrative burdens and improving patient care.
The system has streamlined clinic operations, minimized paperwork, and provided a more efficient workflow for doctors,
nurses, and administrative staff.
