## Overview

**Enola Clinica** is a comprehensive, AI-powered clinic management system that brings together all aspects of healthcare operations — from patient registration, medical records, pharmacy, laboratory, to financial management — into one seamless experience.  

Built with adaptability at its core, Enola Clinica is not limited to traditional electronic medical records (EMR). It can be **customized for different types of healthcare facilities**, including **beauty clinics, aesthetic centers, and hybrid wellness services**, making it a truly modular system that grows with each clinic’s unique needs.

Beyond medical workflows, Enola Clinica also provides **non-medical modules** such as **patient recommendations, loyalty management, and treatment planning** — enabling clinics to strengthen client relationships and deliver personalized experiences alongside clinical care.

---

## Key Features

### **Comprehensive Clinical Management**
- **Multi-Department Support**: Rawat Jalan, Rawat Inap, IGD, Farmasi, Laboratorium, Poli Umum, Poli Gigi, Kebidanan, KIA and Kecantikan.
- **SOAP Documentation**: Structured record system with AI-assisted assessment and planning.
- **Patient History Tracking**: Unified medical and visit records across departments.
- **Appointment & Queue System**: Smart scheduling with queue visibility and real-time updates.

### **AI-Enhanced Intelligence**
- **Smart SOAP Recommendations**: AI analyzes clinician notes to suggest relevant diagnoses, procedures, and prescriptions.
- **Semantic Search Engine**: OpenAI + Pinecone embeddings for intelligent ICD-10, procedure, and drug search.
- **Clinical Insights**: Context-aware recommendations based on previous cases and outcomes.

### **Beauty & Wellness Custom Modules**
- **Aesthetic Procedure Management**: Track aesthetic procedures such as fillers, botox, or laser treatments.
- **Treatment Packages**: Create customizable beauty and skincare treatment bundles.
- **Product Recommendation System**: AI suggests suitable skincare or wellness products based on treatment history.
- **Customer Relationship Management**: Manage leads, follow-ups, and post-treatment communication.

### **Patient Engagement & Retention**
- **Smart Patient Recommendations**: Suggest follow-up treatments or loyalty rewards based on visit history.
- **Membership System**: Flexible membership and discount rules.
- **Notifications & Reminders**: Automatic appointment reminders and health alerts via WhatsApp or email.

### **Pharmacy & Inventory Management**
- **Stock Tracking**: Real-time inventory synchronization across departments.
- **Batch & Expiry Monitoring**: Automatic alerts for expiring medications or consumables.
- **Dynamic Pricing**: Multi-tier pricing for retail, insurance, or member patients.
- **Prescription Integration**: Direct linkage between doctors’ orders and pharmacy dispensing.

### **Finance & Billing**
- **Flexible Billing Engine**: Handles cash, insurance, and mixed payments.
- **Subscription Control**: Built-in subscription system for SaaS billing (inspired by Laravel maintenance mode).
- **Audit & Reporting**: Transparent financial tracking with detailed transaction history.

### **Security & Compliance**
- **Authentication**: Laravel Sanctum with role-based permissions.
- **Audit Trail**: Full data-change logging for accountability.
- **Encryption**: PHI encryption both at rest and in transit.
- **Multi-Tenant Support**: Each clinic’s data is completely isolated and secured.

## Technologies Used

### **Backend**
- **Framework**: Laravel + Filament for robust and modular architecture.
- **Database**: MySQL / MariaDB with optimized indexing for large datasets.
- **Queue Management**: Redis + Horizon for asynchronous background tasks.
- **AI Integration**: OpenAI GPT + Pinecone vector database for semantic search and AI recommendations.
- **File Handling**: DOMPDF for generating printable forms and reports.
- **Storage**: DigitalOcean Block Storage for scalable persistence.

### **Frontend**
- **Framework**: FilamentPHP (Alpine.js + Livewire).
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS with custom Filament themes.
- **Charts & Analytics**: ApexCharts for operational and clinical metrics.
- **Reactive Modals**: Livewire dynamic forms and detail views.
- **Mobile App**: React Native for owners and patients (real-time reporting & booking).

### **Infrastructure**
- **Production**: DigitalOcean Droplet.
- **Development**: VPS environment for staging and internal testing.
- **CI/CD**: GitHub Actions for automated deployments.
- **Monitoring**: Laravel Telescope + custom system health tracking.
- **Backup**: Automated daily backups for database and file storage.

## Customization Flexibility

Enola Clinica is built on a **modular architecture** — each feature can be activated, customized, or extended depending on the clinic’s business model:

- **Medical Clinics** can focus on EMR, SOAP, ICD coding, and lab integrations.  
- **Beauty Clinics** can enable aesthetic procedure modules, client loyalty, and product catalogs.  
- **Hybrid Wellness Centers** can combine both, managing doctors, beauticians, and sales staff in one system.  

Every module (from SOAP to billing) can be customized through internal configuration or white-label adaptation, allowing each facility (faskes) to operate on workflows that match their exact operational reality.

## Challenges and Learnings

### **1. Performance with Complex Relationships**
Managing multi-department data (patients, SOAP, pharmacy, billing) required advanced database optimization and scoped queries.

### **2. Slow ICD-10 Loading**
Heavy datasets caused UI lag — resolved with lazy loading, pagination, and eventually Pinecone vector search.

### **3. Modular Design for Different Clinic Types**
Creating a flexible architecture that could support both medical and beauty workflows required clean separation of models and components.

### **4. AI Workflow Orchestration**
Integrating OpenAI in live clinical environments meant designing efficient caching, retry, and failover logic for uninterrupted use.

### **5. Data Security**
Implemented encrypted storage for sensitive data and isolated tenant environments for SaaS compliance.

## Technical Architecture

### **Backend**
- Layered architecture (Repository → Service → Controller)
- Event-driven actions for audit and logging
- Subscription middleware for usage control
- Queue workers for heavy AI or reporting tasks

### **Frontend**
- Modular Filament resources for scalable UI components
- Reactive Livewire actions and dynamic filtering
- Tailored dashboards for medical and aesthetic use cases

### **AI Pipeline**
1. Embedding generation using OpenAI API  
2. Vector storage in Pinecone for semantic similarity  
3. AI-based recommendations integrated into SOAP and product modules
